My character's name is Io and she's a cyborg. Io lives in the world of the future and learns to live with people, because she lived most of her life apart from them. Despite the fact that she is a cyborg, she can FEEL emotions and HAVE friends. Among them there are people, as well as other cyborgs and even a dinosaur :)

This little robot assistant is Io's best friend.
He is always with her. He knows a lot of things, for example, to drive vehicles, show films, create gallagrams, with which he plays with Io. With his help, she teaches different languages and orientates in the world of people. Io really loves him.

Io loves music, especially rock. She learns to play on her pink guitar and loves to jam with friends.

Io met this dino in the world of people and she immediately fell in love with him. He's so cute, so pink! Dino instilled Io's love for donuts. He likes to sleep very much and he is very curious.

This seemingly sullen boy - in fact he has a very kind heart, without him they all did not meet each other. He is very smart and knows how to build robots. As a child, he lost his hand, so he walks with a cyber hand.

This girl is really cool. Ella (or simply L) is the last person on Earth who does not have artificial implants. She is the most popular DJ in the world, with almost no one knows what she really looks like. L loves old rock and roll, retro movies and vintage cars.

ABOUT ME:

From early childhood, I loved drawing and dreaming
of drawing cartoons, and at school I began to draw
comic books. As a child, I was very inspired by the
anime, especially Sailor Moon and for a very long
time painted in manga style. Probably thanks to the
SM, I love the pastel colors in the drawings :)

Once in the store, I caught a portable Bluetooth speaker
in the form of a small round robot and he liked me so much that
I bought it. His appearance inspired me very much and inspired
fantasies about a futuristic world where these guys fly next to their owners
and perform a bunch of different functions, not just music playback.. I immediately imagined one
of the owner of such a robot assistant and the image of Io came to mind as if by itself. So the first
thing that was invented was her long white dreadlocks. On the idea, these were things that looked
like dreadlocks, but in fact the wires to connect ... connect to what? And then I came up with the idea
that Io is a cyborg who must connect to a huge robot with his dreadlocks..

Coming up this story, I was also inspired by the music of Grimes, Tame Impala, My Morning Jacket,
Bombay Bicycle Club. And yes - cartoons! Steven Universe , Adventure Time and of course,
as always, Sailor Moon <3
